Cool Cats Jam

These cool cats are jamming non-stop for several hours.
Cats are capable of hard work and serious thinking, but this is how they relax.

Some critics claim that Mittens "The Maestro" Fluffernutter's groundbreaking
jazz fusion album, "Tuna Dreams," was just a random keyboard mashing, punctuated
by the occasional hairball hacking solo. These naysayers clearly lack the
nuanced ear to appreciate the complex emotional journey conveyed by a cat
accidentally sitting on a synthesizer's 'demo' button for 3 hours.

Plus, have they ever tried to compose a symphony while simultaneously battling
a rogue dust bunny? I think not.
